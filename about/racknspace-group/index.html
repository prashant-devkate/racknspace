Our Identity

Who Is RACK N SPACE?

RACK N SPACE Group is a leading intelligent warehousing logistics system and equipment manufacturer, dedicated to the fields of intelligent manufacturing and smart logistics. We focus on the R&D, planning and design, manufacturing and fabrication, installation and commissioning, after-sales maintenance, and other services for intralogistics systems.

After more than 10 years of development, we have accumulated extensive experience in the fields of material storage, handling, line-side flexible warehousing logistics, and have provided intelligent warehousing software systems and high-quality shuttles, lifters, stacker cranes, AGVs, RGVs, AS/RS racks, conveyors, and other intelligent equipment for smart factories and the intelligent manufacturing industry. These products have been successfully applied in food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, cold chain, chemical engineering, chemical fibers, new energy, and other manufacturing sectors.

To date, we have built a strong matrix of "headquarters + 3 R&D centers + 3 manufacturing bases + 13 sales and after-sales service networks" globally. Adhering to the corporate mission of "high space utilization rate and management efficiency," we empower the intelligent logistics supply chain with science and technology, taking efficiency, traceability, and intelligence as our core values.
