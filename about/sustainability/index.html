Our Principles

Environmental POLICY

RackNSpace endeavors to conduct its business activities in an environmentally friendly and responsible manner. We recognize that sustainable operations are vital to the future of smart manufacturing and global logistics.

Our intelligent warehousing systems are designed not only to optimize space and efficiency but also to minimize energy consumption and reduce the overall carbon footprint of our clients' operations. By integrating advanced software with energy-efficient hardware, we ensure that modern supply chains can scale responsibly.

From the materials we source for our heavy-duty racking to the software algorithms optimizing shuttle routes, environmental consciousness is built directly into our engineering process. We strictly adhere to structural guidelines and international standards to ensure a greener tomorrow.

The 5 Pillars

Guiding Principles

01

Fully embody the business principle of environmental protection in the company's daily operations and long-term strategic decisions.

02

Dynamically identify environmental risks and take prudent, effective measures to prevent pollution and reduce resource burden in manufacturing.

03

Comply rigorously with all applicable laws and regulations related to environmental protection and intelligent warehousing infrastructure.

04

Effectively implement and continuously improve our environmental management system to establish a sustainable operating model.

05

Willfully receive and share appropriate training and guidance to consistently meet our own and our partners' environmental requirements.

Our Commitment

To actively contribute to the conservation of air, water, soil, and natural resources, and to integrate sustainability as a core element of our long-term business strategy.
