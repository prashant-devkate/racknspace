Environmental Policy

RackNSpace endeavors to conduct its business activities in an environmentally friendly and responsible manner in accordance with the following guidelines:

Fully embody the business principle of environmental protection in the company's operations.

Dynamically identify environmental risks and take prudent and effective measures to prevent pollution and reduce the burden on the environment and resources in the company's production and operation processes.

Comply with the laws and regulations applicable to the company's operations, and comply with relevant environmental protection regulations.

Effectively implement and continuously improve our environmental management system, including the establishment of a sustainable operating model to achieve environmental goals and objectives.

We are willing to receive and share appropriate training and guidance to meet our own and our partners' environmental requirements.
