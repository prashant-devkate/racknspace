About Us

RACK N SPACE Group is a smart warehousing logistics system and equipment integrator that is dedicated in the field of intelligent manufacturing and smart logistics. The company focuses on the R&D, planning & design, equipment integration, installation & commissioning, after-sales maintenance and other services of intralogistics system.

Currently, RACK N SPACE has established a robust global service network, including its headquarters, three R&D centers, three manufacturing bases, three overseas support centers and national wide after-sales service centers. With over 1,000 clients benefiting from RACK N SPACE's logistics solutions and products.
